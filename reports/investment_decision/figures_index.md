# Investment Decision Figures Index

These figures support Steps 1–5b of the investment decision analysis.

## Steps 1–3

- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step1_top_candidate_revenue.png`: ranks the top budget-feasible segments by median annual revenue.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step1_best_segment_by_city.png`: compares each city's strongest feasible segment.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step2_knn_validation_comparison.png`: compares segment medians with k-NN comparable-listing medians.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step3_revenue_scenarios.png`: shows conservative, moderate, and optimistic revenue cases.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step3_bootstrap_median_uncertainty.png`: shows bootstrap uncertainty around median revenue.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step3_top_candidate_sensitivity.png`: stress-tests the current top candidate.

## Step 4 — Risk and seasonality

- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step4_risk_score_by_candidate.png`: composite risk score by candidate segment/city.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step4_risk_components_heatmap.png`: decomposition of risk drivers.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step4_city_seasonality.png`: seasonal demand patterns by city.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step4_monthly_occupancy_by_city.png`: monthly occupancy rate by city.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step4_monthly_occupancy_index_by_city.png`: monthly occupancy indexed to each city's average.

## Step 5 — Portfolio diversification

- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step5_portfolio_revenue_vs_risk.png`: revenue versus risk for portfolio scenarios.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step5_recommended_portfolios.png`: recommended multi-city portfolios.
- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step5_city_occupancy_correlation.png`: cross-city occupancy correlation (diversification context).

## Step 5b — Efficient frontier

- `reports/figures/05_investment_decision/step5b_efficient_frontier.png`: risk–return efficient frontier for portfolio weights.
